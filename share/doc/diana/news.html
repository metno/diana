      RRR :
      At 06 and 18: precipitation past 12 hours
      At 00 and 12: precipitation past 6 hours
      Else precipitation past 1 hour
      (In datatype synop-obs, accumulation time is not well defined)

      At 00, 06, 12 and 18: max wind gust past 6 hours
      At 03, 09, 15 and 21: max wind gust past 3 hours
      Else wind gust past 1 hour

      At 03, 09, 15 and 21: max wind gust past 3 hours
